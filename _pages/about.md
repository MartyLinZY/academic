I am a software engineering student at Nanjing University. My work focuses on software testing and reliability, particularly for distributed systems and deep learning frameworks.

Research interests
======

- Testing and debugging distributed systems
- Deterministic reproduction of distributed-system failures
- Differential testing for deep learning frameworks and operators
- Static analysis and fault localization

Selected work
======

**RediI** is a test infrastructure for deterministically reproducing failures in distributed systems. The work was published at ICSE 2025. [Publication details]({{ base_path }}/publication/redii/) · [Source code](https://github.com/SSCT-Lab/RediI)
